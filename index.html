Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24.12.2024

At Serenity, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we may collect when you use our service, how we handle and store that information, and your rights regarding your data. By using Serenity, you agree to the practices described in this policy. 1. Information We Collect We do not collect personal information such as names, emails, or device identifiers. However, when you use Serenity, you may upload files such as .ipa files or mobileconfig files, which may be stored temporarily. These files are used solely for the purpose of signing or installation and are deleted after a certain period. 2. How We Collect Data Data is collected in the following ways: Through APIs: We use APIs to interact with your uploaded files (e.g., signing .ipa files). Local Storage: Your app data and temporary files may be saved locally on your device for user convenience. 3. Third-Party Services Serenity uses third-party APIs for file signing, but we do not directly control or collect any data from these services. You should review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle data. 4. Data Storage & Security We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of any files you upload, but we do not store personal information. Files uploaded via the app are stored temporarily and deleted after a short period for security and privacy reasons. Local Storage: Any data saved locally (e.g., temporary files) is stored on your device and is not shared or transmitted to external servers unless specified in the app functionality. 5. Data Sharing We do not share your data with third parties. The files you upload are processed through APIs and deleted once they are no longer needed. 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies Serenity does not use cookies, tracking technologies, or advertising networks to collect user data. 7. User Rights Since we do not collect personal information, users do not have specific rights over personal data. However, you may download and save files stored locally on your device. All uploaded files are deleted automatically after processing. 8. Use of Our Service Serenity is available for use by individuals of all ages, provided they understand and agree to the terms of the service. While the app is accessible to all, we do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. 9. Data Deletion All files uploaded to Serenity are processed and deleted after a set period. The app does not retain user files unless stored locally on your device for your convenience. 10. International Use Serenity is available at serenityios.com. While we do not specifically target users in regions governed by privacy laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), we recommend that users understand the privacy implications of using third-party APIs, as Serenity itself does not collect personal data. 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data. 12. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your use of Serenity, please contact us at Telegram/TikTok/Discord, You can find our Socials in the Settings.
